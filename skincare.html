Tips for oily Skin...

    🍋Wash face every morning, evening and after exercise
    🍋Choose skin products that are labeled "oil free" and "noncomedogenic "
    🍋Use a soft face cloth to wash face
    🍋Apply moisturizer daily
    🍋Wear sunscreen outdoors- look for sunscreen that contain zinc oxide and titanium dioxide. Do not use sunscreens that contain fragrance or oils.
    🍋Choose oil free and water based makeup
    🍋Do not sleep in your makeup
    🍋Try to use blotting papers through out the day- gently press the paper to your face don't rub it or it will spread the oils across your face
    🍋Don't touch your face throughout the day
