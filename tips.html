Tips & Tricks

Skincare

When doing skincare...

🎀 Always clean your hands before doing skincare (Even before washing your face)

🎀 Don't apply facewash directly onto your skin (Put the product on your palm and rub it until it becomes a fine foam. Appply the foam on your face and gently rub.)

🎀 Don't use a towel to wipe your face. Use a tissue instead.

🎀 Don't touch your face unless you cleaned your hands.

🎀 Use sunscreen every day, even on cloudy days.

🎀 Clean makeup brushes, sponges, and other skincare tools to prevent bacteria buildup. (Including your pillowcase)

🎀 Exfoliate 1-3 times a week depending on your skin type.( Use a physical scrub or a chemical exfoliant (like AHAs or BHAs) to remove dead skin cells.)

🎀 Use cold water instead of hot when washing your face. (Cold water can help close pores and improve blood circulation.)

🎀 Carry a face mist for quick hydration throughout the day.


Haircare

Hair oiling...

🌼 Select an oil based on your hair type

🌼 Start with dry hair. (If your hair is excessively dirty or has product buildup, consider washing it first and letting it dry completely.)

🌼 Part your hair into sections (typically 4-6 sections) for easier application.(Use hair clips to secure the sections.)

🌼 Take a small amount of oil on your fingertips. Start applying it from the scalp to the ends of your hair. Use your fingers to massage the oil into your scalp in circular motions.

🌼 Once fully applied, cover your hair with a shower cap or a warm towel.

🌼 Don't let the oil sit for more than 5 hours

🌼 DO NOT leave the oil overnight!!

Shampooing...

🌺 Rinse your hair with warm water to open up the cuticles.

🌺 Pour a small amount (about a quarter-sized) of shampoo into your palm. Rub your hands together to distribute it before applying.

🌺 Apply the shampoo to your scalp and gently massage with your fingertips (not nails).

🌺 Avoid shampooing the length and ends of your hair.

🌺 Rinse your hair completely with cool water to close the cuticles.

🌺 If your hair is particularly dirty or oily, repeat the shampooing process.

🌺 Apply a suitable conditioner to the lengths and ends of your hair. (Wait about 10 minutes before rinse.)

🌺 Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water after conditioning.

Afterwash care...

🍀 Use a soft, absorbent towel to gently pat your hair dry.(Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously)

🍀 Use a leave-in conditioner or a lightweight hair serum.

🍀 If you plan to use heat styling tools apply a heat protectant spray or serum.

🍀 Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently remove knots. (Start from the ends and work your way up.)

🍀 Let your hair dry naturally for minimal damage or use a blow dryer in lowest heat level.

🍀 After styling, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair.

🍀 Avoid Tight Hairstyles. (Use hair ties that are gentle on your hair)

🍀 Before bed, loosely braide your hair or put it in a loose bun to prevent tangling. Use a silk or satin pillowcase
